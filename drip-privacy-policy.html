Drip AI – Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Drip AI (the “App”) handles information when you use the App.

Developer: Teodor Voznyy

1. Photos Uploaded by Users

When you use the App you may upload a photo from your camera or photo library.

The photo is transmitted securely via HTTPS to our backend to generate an AI image.

Photos are processed temporarily and are not permanently stored on the server.

2. AI Image Generation

Photos are processed using artificial intelligence services to generate stylized images based on the user’s input.

These images are generated automatically and may not perfectly represent the original person or scene.

3. Generated Images

Generated images are saved only on the user’s device if the user chooses to save them.

The App does not automatically store generated images on its servers.

4. In-App Purchases

Purchases and subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store.

We do not collect or store payment information such as credit card numbers.

Subscription status may be verified using RevenueCat to unlock paid features.

5. App Preferences

The App may store limited preferences locally on the device such as onboarding status.

These preferences remain on the device and are not transmitted to our servers.

6. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Contacts from your device
  • Advertising identifiers

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services including:

  • Google Gemini (AI processing)
  • Supabase (backend infrastructure)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Apple App Store (payments)

These providers may process data in countries outside the European Union.

8. Data Security

We use secure HTTPS connections and rely on trusted infrastructure providers to help protect transmitted data.

9. User Responsibilities

Users must ensure they have permission to upload photos of other individuals.

The App must not be used to create deceptive or harmful deepfake content or to impersonate other individuals.

10. Children

The App is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
